# JourneyOS Time Boundary & Future Journey System V1

## Core Rule

JourneyOS must separate:

- Past: real Moments that already happened.
- Now: the Moment currently being lived.
- Future: Journey plans that have not happened yet.

Future must never pretend to be memory.

## Boundary

The current local date is the boundary.

- Dates before today belong to Memory Space.
- Today belongs to Now Moment.
- Dates after today belong to Future Journey.

Future days may contain plans, routes, hotels, restaurants, transport, reminders, and suggestions.
Future days must not contain Memory, Photo Memory, Story, or Reflection.

## Lifecycle

Idea -> Plan -> Confirmed -> Now -> Memory

When a future day arrives, it can become a Now Moment.
Only after it is lived can it become Memory.

## AI Boundary

AI must not create fake memories.
AI may only assist as an intelligence layer:

- Parse future plans.
- Suggest possible future journeys.
- Refine user-created Moment text.
- Organize real memories into stories.

AI generated content must be marked as generated and must never overwrite lived experience.

## Final Principle

Do not manufacture memory.
Do not predict memory.
Do not confuse future with lived life.

JourneyOS preserves what truly happened.
The future only waits to happen.
